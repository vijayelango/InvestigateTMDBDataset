Introduction¶

In this project, I will be analyzing TMDB Movies dataset.
After looking at the data set breifly in a spreadsheet, following questions came to my mind.
I will analyse the dataset and provisionally answer the below questions

1. Does the budget of the movie determine its success (revenue collected)?
2. Was there a raise in popularity of the movies over the years? Did this raise help increase in revenues?
3. When is the best time to release a movie?
4. Which director should a production house approach to earn more revenues?
5. Are movies of certain genre popular during certain months?

Conclusions¶


I was able analyse the TMDB dataset and answer questions that I posed at the start of the lesson.
All the findings are based on this dataset alone and are hence provisional

1. Does the budget of the movie determine its success (revenue collected)?
The budget with which the movie was made determines its success (in terms of revenue).
Movies that were made on a larger budget collected more revenues and vice-versa.

2. Was there a raise in popularity of the movies over the years? Did this raise help increase in revenues?
There was an uptick in the number of movies made and popularity of the movies over the years.
Production houses made larger revenues due to increased # of movies.

3. When is the best time to release a movie?
By looking at average revenues made per month, we could deduce movies released during Summer and Holiday seasons made more revenues.

4. Which director should a production house approach to earn more revenues?
Looking at the average revenues collected by a movie that the director directed (taking number of movies also in to account), we were able to get the list of top performers.
This list is based on historical performance that a production can use for reference.
It cannot be used to measure future performance.

5. Are movies of certain genre popular during certain months?
We could see movies of different genre were popular during different month. But in general science fiction were the most popular followed by animation and fantasy.
